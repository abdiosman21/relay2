The Sources script

Drop one script. See every company on your site.

Sources is Relay's visitor identification script. Drop it on your site, and every business visitor is de-anonymised in real time: company name, firmographics, visited pages, scroll depth, and a propensity score. Matched to target accounts, routed to your SDR queue, surfaced in your pipeline.

  • Privacy
    B2B only, consent-forward. Only identifies business IPs with reverse-DNS firmographics. Personal device identification is opt-in with a clear banner, GDPR and Australian Privacy Act aligned.
  • Routing
    Auto-route by rule. High-intent pages + target account → SDR agent. Support pages → service agent. Careers page → ignored.
  • Attribution
    First-touch, always. Every deal tied back to the first Sources visit. No UTMs required.

Forecasting

The forecast that already matches your ad spend target.

Every revenue team forecasts twice: bottoms-up from account owners, and top-down from a number the CFO or the ads platform is holding them to. Relay runs both, in the same view, and surfaces the gap automatically. Connect Google Ads, Xero, Stripe, or QuickBooks once, and the top-down number updates daily.

  • Bottoms-up
    Per-account forecasting. Owners commit a quarterly or annual spend forecast on every account. Rolled up by segment, owner, region, or product line in one click.
  • Top-down
    Connected to your ad + finance stack. Google Ads gives you a daily revenue goal. Xero and Stripe give you recurring and invoiced revenue. Relay reconciles both against pipeline and tells you the gap.
  • Forecast Agent
    Writes the commentary. Every Friday: what moved, why, where the risk is, and which three actions close the gap. In plain English. Attached to the weekly report.

Questions

The short answers.

How is this different from HubSpot adding AI?
HubSpot's AI (Breeze) is bolted onto a product that was architected in 2014 around Hubs, contact tiers, and a partner ecosystem. Matching Relay's pricing would require HubSpot to cannibalise the partner channel that is currently its largest distribution asset. We don't have that constraint because we don't have a partner channel.
How does your forecasting compare to Clari?
Clari is a forecasting tool that sits on top of your CRM. Relay's Forecast agent lives inside the CRM and reads directly from pipeline, Google Ads spend targets, Stripe ARR, and Xero invoices. You get the same bottoms-up rollup, plus the top-down target reconciliation, plus the plain-English commentary, without the US$85k Clari bill.
Do I still need PandaDoc or DocuSign?
No. Relay Documents is a native proposal + contract builder with section-level read tracking and legally binding e-sign (SOC 2 Type II, AES-256). When your proposal is opened by a new stakeholder, the deal risk score updates automatically. No integration fee, no per-envelope pricing.
How does Sources compare to RB2B or Clearbit Reveal?
Functionally similar for the core de-anonymisation job; the difference is that Sources sits inside the CRM. Visitor signals automatically become lead scores, routing rules, SDR tasks, and first-touch attribution, with no Zapier glue. It's bundled into every Relay plan, not a US$395 per month line item.
Can the Implementation Agent really replace a partner?
For SMB migrations (under 25 users), yes, in 72 hours, flat US$2,500. For mid-market (under 250 users), yes, in about two weeks, flat US$9,500, with a human-in-the-loop review flow. Complex regulated migrations still benefit from a named implementation lead, which is included in the Enterprise plan.
What about data sovereignty and privacy?
Multi-region data residency (US, EU, AU), SOC 2 Type II at GA, DPA on request, and a zero-retention inference mode. Every agent action is logged with full provenance and can be reverted. Sources only identifies business IPs by default; personal-device identification is opt-in with a clear banner.
